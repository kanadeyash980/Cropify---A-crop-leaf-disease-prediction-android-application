In order to guarantee global food security and economic stability, agriculture isessential. 
Crop diseases, however, pose a serious danger to crop productivity andquality and are just one of the many difficulties the agricultural industry mustovercome. 
To lessen these dangers, early detection and prompt intervention arenecessary. 
A cutting-edge crop disease prediction application for android to helpfarmers and agricultural stakeholders efficiently identify and manage crop diseases is developed.
In order to deliver precise and immediate detection of crop illnesses, the Crop Disease Prediction Application makes use of the power of Convolutional Neural Network. 
With the help of the application, farmers can take pictures of infected crops on their Android handsets, which are then analysed using convolutional neural network. 
By analysing the photos and comparing them to a huge database of crop disease patterns, the proposed system enables quick diagnosis of the disease impacting potato, tomato, apple and corn crops. The accuracy obtained is 98% forpotato, 95% for tomato, 91% for apple and 92% for corn respectively.
